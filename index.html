About Me

Born and raised in China, in 2011 I came to the US for high school when I was 16. Having lived in different places in the world and embraced various cultures, I developed a strong desire for innovations and challenges. I aspire to apply the power of technology to create value, so my long term goal is to become a technology entrepreneur and to deliver impactful technical products.

I double majored in Computer Science and Economics at Colgate University, a top liberal arts college located in a beautiful town called Hamilton. I just recently graduated with Magna Cum Laude and High Honors in Computer Science. My interests include programming, playing guitar, filmmaking and sports. Some of my technical and filmmaking projects are shown below.
